Celestial

Simple, softening best-seller

Vegan

Who’s it for?

Stressed-out complexions will love our bestselling moisturiser for sensitive skin. It’s formulated to be especially gentle whilst delivering the hydration a healthy skin barrier needs.

What’s in it for you?

A simple, soothing blend of vanilla water, organic cocoa butter and over 5% almond milk, Celestial carefully shields delicate skin from all that life (and you) throw at it. Sensitive skin may feel tight or inflamed, and your face, which is home to a high density of nerve endings and has a thinner skin barrier than that of your body, is especially susceptible to discomfort. That’s why we’ve packed this moisturiser with the mildest natural ingredients we could find to help your skin barrier function and feel lovely again.

Over 15% almond oil is combined with over 5% almond milk and vanilla water for their antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, helping to soothe your complexion and support your skin’s ability to care for itself. Vegan glycerine and Fair Trade organic cocoa butter soften the surface of your complexion and reduce moisture loss, helping boost the effectiveness of your skin barrier against pollutants and external irritants. It’s a light, simple blend that sinks in quickly to fortify your skin and there’s a reason it’s been a bestseller since 1996.

What’s in it for the planet?

The vanilla inside is made from pods from small-holder farms in Uganda and Tanzania and a Madagascan company promoting agroforestry practices, vanilla farming in these regions is boosting the incomes of local farmers and the biodiversity of East African forests. The Fair Trade and organic cocoa butter inside is also 100% traceable from sources in Sierra Leone, Dominican Republic and Peru where agroforestry models are in practice to protect local forests.

How to use: Cleanse, tone and use clean hands to gently sweep this vegan moisturiser over your skin. Store in cool, dry conditions. Best used fresh.

Ingredients: Vanilla Water, Almond Oil, Almond Milk, Glycerine, Fair Trade Organic Cocoa Butter, Vanilla Pod Extract, Stearic Acid, Cetearyl Alcohol, Triethanolamine, Methylparaben, Propylparaben
